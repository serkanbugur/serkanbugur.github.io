Current Research

My research aim is to understand and model the development of manipulation actions such as grasping, pushing, etc. in the early infancy. I would like to answer the following questions for this purpose.

  1. Why does an infant perform manipulation actions ?
  2. What are the underlying mechanisms for the action selection in the exploration phase ?
  3. How do we model the action understanding and execution of infants ? And how to transfer the knowledge gained ?
